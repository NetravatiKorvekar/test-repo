ABOUT BLENZE SALON

BLENZE Salon, India's first and leading chain of beauty salons, offers
expert services in hairstyling, skin and beauty care. It is dedicated to
the contemporary Indian woman's exploration of the fine art of beauty.
Known for our magical concoction of stunning make-up, fabulous hair and
excellent skin services, we bring the backstage expertise and experience
of BLENZE Fashion Week to modern Indian women through a team of over 3500+
highly trained professional stylists. BLENZE Salons are dedicated towards
the evolvement of the modern Indian woman and her exploration of beauty.
Professionally trained hair and makeup experts with countless shows under
our belt and outstanding skin services come together at BLENZE Salon and
offer an unforgettable experience.
